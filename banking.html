BANKING GUIDES FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

Requirements for opening a UK Bank Account - Physical and Online Banking.

OPENING A BANK IN UK

Information about how to set up your UK bank account is included in the Welcome Toolkit you will have access to on CampusMoodle, once you have enrolled online. This information includes how to set up a UK bank account whilst in self-isolation.

You can access this information from RGU Website

Please Note: You will also need your passport/photo ID card.
A 'bank support letter' from the University confirming your registration, a prove of address in the UK and your overseas address and the fact that the University has corresponded with you at your overseas address.
