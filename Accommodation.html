Getting Accommodation

There are different types of accommodation right here in Scotland, it could be accommodation with family (2 bedroom flat, 1 room for rent etc.)
private student accommodation (Ensuite or non-Ensuite) or school accommodation of which RGU got you covered. Its important that you feel secured and comforting staying in Aberdeen, Scotland.

Type Of Accommodation

  1. RGU Accommodation (Student Halls)
  2. Local Rent Accommodation
  3. Private Student Accommodation
  4. Temporary Accommodation (such as Hotels, airbnb etc.)

RGU Accommodation

Robert Gordon University has a few student student hall accommodation which are of various cost rentage,
the student hall are mostly ensuite and not suitable for families. They include;

  • Crathie Student Village (ranges from £152 - £162 per week)
  • Ramsay Development ( same cost range as the above )
  • Garthee Towers ( £122 per week)
  • Woolmanhill Flats (ranging from £85 - £133 per week.)

Local Rent Accommodation

Local rent accommodation are accommodation that can be gotten through third parties, such as agents and various Scottish landlords. These accommodation can be found online through various house rentage platform such as Zoopla, this platform is mostly suited for students coming with their families. The cost of a bedroom apartment in aberdeen is between £270 - £800 per month depending on the kind of facilities the house has and it's location. Another online platform is SpareRoom
SpareRoom is used mostly by student or landlords who have extra bedroom to share or rent in a building which there are occupant already, it is mostly for single students and a more cheaper option. Other platform that you can acquire a local rent accommodation include;

  • Right Move
  • On the Market
  • Open Rent

Note
- Before you make any payment to an agent, landlords, please ensure you view the house which you are paying for so as not to do away with your hard earned money.



Private Student Accommodation

Private student accommodations are accommodations that offer accommodation services to only students, it could be ensuite rooms, non ensuite or a studio apartment. They are a little bit expensive but are mostly convenient for student experience.You are also allow to book for an accommodationbefore you arrive, so your room is secured before landing. Some of them include;

  • Spring Gardens, Aberdeen
  • Farmers Hall Lodge
  • Caledon Court, Aberdeen
  • Mealmarket Exchange etc.
