RGU Societies: how to join or set up your own

For RGU's official societies site visit https://www.rguunion.co.uk/getinvolved/societies/

How to join a society

  1. follow the link RGU Societies
  2. follow the link RGU Sports Clubs
  3. Sign in with your RGU credentials
  4. Click on the Society/Sports Club you would like to join
  5. Each link will show you a "join" button. click to join
  6. Fill out your details and pay the fees

thats it!
