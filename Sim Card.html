Getting access to sim cards

The necessity of buying a UK sim card at London Heathrow airport

As a student, it is advisable to buy a sim card immediately after you arrive at the airport. Buying a Prepaid UK sim card can help you save on roaming cost within the UK and depending on the sim card, possibly within the EU. Whether you’re staying in the UK or passing through Heathrow on your way to Europe this guide will help you pick up a sim card at the airport. Before travelling, make sure your phone is able to use UK Sim cards. You’ll need to make sure your phone is unlocked or contact your phone network to ask for an unlock code. Depending on the phone and where you live, there may also be a store or online website that can help.

Sim cards at RGU

Sim cards can be gotten at the RGU Student Help point for FREE! EE & Lebara sim card providers can be found at this help point

Getting a sim card at Heathrow airport

Some local stores in Heathrow airport located in Terminal 2, 3, and 5 sell sim cards.
These stores are open from 6am to 10pm. At these stores, you can easily get help from the sales representatives if you are finding it difficult to determine which plan you need.
